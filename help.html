Getting Started

Creating an account is easy! Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner, enter your email address, create a password, and verify your email. You can also sign up using your Google, Facebook, or Apple account for faster registration.

Yes! PopFusion offers a free tier with access to millions of songs, basic discovery features, and community functions. However, free users will hear ads between songs and have limited skips. Upgrade to Premium for an ad-free experience with unlimited features.

After creating your account, you'll be guided through a setup process where you can select your favorite genres, artists, and songs. This helps our AI recommendation system understand your taste and suggest music you'll love.

Premium & Billing

Premium includes ad-free listening, unlimited skips, high-quality audio (320kbps), offline downloads, advanced AI recommendations, and exclusive content. You also get early access to new features and premium-only playlists.

Premium costs $9.99/month or $7.99/month if you pay annually. We also offer a Family plan for $14.99/month that covers up to 6 accounts. Students can get Premium for $4.99/month with valid student ID verification.

Absolutely! You can cancel your Premium subscription at any time through your account settings. You'll continue to have Premium access until the end of your current billing period, then your account will automatically switch to the free tier.

Music & Playback

Check your internet connection first. If you're connected but still having issues, try refreshing the page or restarting the app. For Premium users, ensure you haven't exceeded the device limit. Contact support if the problem persists.

Click the "+" button next to any song to add it to a playlist. You can create new playlists or add to existing ones. To share, go to your playlist, click the share button, and choose how you want to share (link, social media, etc.).

Go to Settings > Audio Quality. Free users can choose between Normal (128kbps) and High (256kbps). Premium users get access to Very High quality (320kbps) for the best listening experience.
