Privacy

Privacy expectations for a static prototype.

PopFusion is currently a front-end concept. The privacy page should reflect that honestly instead of implying a larger live platform than what exists.

Quick note

Demo account data lives only in your own browser. Newsletter and support forms are prototype interactions and do not connect to a live backend.

What data the prototype can store

  • Local browser storage for the demo sign-in flow.
  • Temporary form entries handled in-page for validation and feedback.
  • No real server-side account or payment storage in this prototype pass.

What the site does not currently do

  • No real payment processing.
  • No live newsletter subscription backend.
  • No production analytics pipeline that identifies individual users.

How to ask privacy questions

If this concept moves into a live product, the privacy scope will need a fuller rewrite. For now, use the Plan Visit support page for prototype questions or feedback.

The privacy page now matches the actual scope of the prototype instead of overstating live capabilities.
