Terms

Prototype terms should be short, accurate, and honest.

These terms describe how the current static concept should be understood: as a design and UX prototype, not a fully operational festival platform.

Scope

No purchase, ticketing, or backend service is created through this prototype. Any forms or account flows are demonstrations only.

Prototype use

  • The site is provided for coursework, concept validation, and design review.
  • Content, lineup names, and operational details may be illustrative rather than final.
  • Links to external services are for reference and exploration only.

Accounts and forms

  • Registration and sign-in are browser-only demonstrations.
  • Newsletter and support forms show intended behavior but do not guarantee delivery to a real inbox.
  • No paid subscription or contractual access model is active in this version.

Questions

If you need clarification on the prototype scope or how a page is intended to behave, use the Help page or the Plan Visit support flow.
